Your Dinner Deals - About Us

Your Dinner Deals has been created by two young web entrepreneurs. They are always talking passionately about new products to be more efficient and love sharing great dinners together.
The idea of Your Dinner Deals came naturaly realizing they were spending way too much time looking for nice new places at a discount on all the different services available.

YDD is designed to save you a lot of time and send you directly the best deals that fits your tastes.
From the restaurants that have the best ratings in the different services available : Yelp, Foursquare, TripAdvisor... we handpick the ones that currently have a good discount you can enjoy.




Your Dinner Deals is a startup currently working hard to build its product and expand its service to many other cities.
We always look for the best talents. If you want to join YDD write to jobs@yourdinnerdeals.com
